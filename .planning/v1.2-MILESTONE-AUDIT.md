### Integration Findings (Tech Debt, Not Blockers)

#### INT-01 — Tooltip Text Decoupled from legal-articles.json (affects VERI-01, VERI-02)
**Severity:** Medium (maintenance risk)
**Finding:** Tooltip text in `public/index.html` (7 hardcoded spans) was manually authored. It is NOT read from `data/legal-articles.json` at build time. `generate-pages.js` has zero references to `legal-articles.json`.
**Consequence:** When Phase 9's `check-bcn-changes.py` detects a BCN legal text change and updates `legal-articles.json`, the user-facing tooltip text will remain stale until `index.html` is manually updated.
**Also:** The `feriado_dia_trabajo` tooltip cites "Ley 19.973" which does not appear in `legal-articles.json`.
**Recommendation:** Document this manual update procedure in BLUEPRINT.md, OR add a `generate-pages.js` step that reads `legal-articles.json` to inject tooltip snippets.

#### INT-02 — feriado_dia_trabajo articulo_numero Inconsistency (affects CHNG-01)
**Severity:** Low (one claim may be silently skipped)
**Finding:** `data/legal-articles.json` stores `feriado_dia_trabajo` with `"articulo_numero": "1"` (numeric). All other `bcn-ley-2977` entries use `"PRIMERO"`. The `find_article_text()` alias matching uses `'1' in art_name_lower` — `'1' in 'articulo primero'` is `False`, so this claim may silently fail lookup in future `check-bcn-changes.py` runs.
**Recommendation:** Normalize `feriado_dia_trabajo.articulo_numero` from `"1"` to `"PRIMERO"` in `data/legal-articles.json`.
